# Research Brief

## Purpose

Turn a vague problem or innovation idea into a bounded research brief so downstream benchmarking and recommendation are focused and decision-useful.

## Quality Checks

- [ ] Research question is clear
- [ ] User roles and job-to-be-done are stated
- [ ] Scope / out-of-scope are explicit
- [ ] Required decision outputs are listed
